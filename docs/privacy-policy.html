Echo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

Echo is a Chrome extension that provides AI-generated expert perspectives on YouTube video content. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data We Collect

Echo collects and processes the following data locally on your device:

  • YouTube video metadata — video title, channel name, and transcript text from the currently active YouTube page. This data is used solely to generate relevant expert responses.
  • Session storage — temporary data stored via chrome.storage.session to maintain state while the extension is active. This data is automatically cleared when the browser is closed.
  • Soul Dex entries — expert responses you choose to save are stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local.

Data We Send

When you interact with a video through Echo, the video title and transcript are sent to our secure API proxy (hosted on Cloudflare Workers) to generate an AI expert response. The API proxy forwards the request to the Qwen language model. We do not store or log the content of these requests beyond what is necessary for rate limiting.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not use cookies or analytics trackers
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not collect data from any website other than youtube.com

Permissions

Echo requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — to read video content from the current YouTube tab
  • sidePanel — to display the Echo panel alongside YouTube
  • storage — to save your Soul Dex entries locally
  • host_permissions (youtube.com) — to operate on YouTube pages

Third-Party Services

Echo uses a Cloudflare Worker as an API proxy to communicate with the Qwen AI model (provided by Alibaba Cloud). Requests are subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy and Alibaba Cloud's Terms of Service.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
